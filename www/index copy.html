Scholarship Academy

eveRed is looking for new players who want to become eR scholars. The first step is to apply to the Academy program. If you are selected, you will be able to join the academy and prove your worth during one month. We offer guidance to help you maximise your SLP and victories in Arena. By the end of the 30 days trial, we will evaluate your performance and decide to promote you as scholar or not. The key performance items evaluated will be your potential for farm SLP and rating in Arena.
